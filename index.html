Pig Game

The Pig Game, a fun and interactive 2-player game built mainly with JavaScript. In this game, players take turns rolling dice to accumulate points. Players can choose to hold their points at any time to prevent losing them, but if they roll a 1, they lose all their accumulated points for that turn. The first player to reach 100 points wins the game. With its simple yet engaging gameplay, the Pig Game is a great way to spend time with friends

Digital Clock

Digital Clock project, a visually stunning and fully functional digital clock built mainly using JavaScript. The clock features a clean and modern design, displaying the current time, date and seconds.
